American Society of Mechanical Engineers

  • Who are the members of this society?
    • Size of Membership: 130,000 members
    • Who are the Members: 32,000 are students, while the remaining majority are professional engineers.
  • Why does this association exist?
    • Services: ASME provides a wide range of services which include continuing education, job training, and even research.
  • Becoming a member
    • I would join this organization if I were interested in Mechanical Engineering as a future profession.
    • It would be useful as an engineer because it gives you a wide array of connections.
    • You have the opportunity to use the programs listed above.

I was fortunate to grow up in the city where I did, and I took every opportunity that came to me. I am a hardworking and determined student who refuses to fail and strives to overachieve.
